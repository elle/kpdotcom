What do I offer?

Based on the NSW Central Coast in Australia, I offer software development services. I specialise in developing custom business software, often in the form of dynamic web applications, using languages such as Ruby and Java. I also offer services in development coaching and technology mentoring.

What is my approach to Software Development?

Good software provides benefits to people. Good software developers strive to collaborate with others to create better software and enable people to make better use of it. These simple principles underpin my approach to software development, which is based on more than 20 years of professional experience.
